Abstract

Archie S. - February 9, 2018 11:14 am

My senior research project is intended to study facial expressions through artificial neural networks. Our expressions are oftentimes indicators of the emotions we are feeling. To this end, the ability for a system to automatically determine a person’s emotional state. Particularly, this has applications in senior care – where constant monitoring of the patient’s well-being is necessary.

By creating a macro that draws images from video data (through computer vision), faces can be fed into a neural network trained from a database of various facial expressions. Via optimization (perhaps through neuroevolution), a real-time emotion recognition system is hoped to be developed.
